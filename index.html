About

We are on a quest to make the Due Diligence Easy

Keeper is a multi-layer platform that allows you to customize the documents for your Due Diligence. It was built upon the most common requirements asked by angel investors and VCs in Europe and in the US.

By taking the most common documents and processes tackled by investors, we were able to automate many of the processes so you could save time constructing the Due Diligence folder.
